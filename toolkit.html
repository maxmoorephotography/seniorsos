The toolkit

Six helpers, in plain English.

Each one takes a real, everyday problem and answers it the way a patient, tech-savvy relative would — no jargon, no ten-step tutorials.

Free

Scam Shield

Bill Buster

Feed in a confusing bill — electricity, water, phone, council rates — and get back what it actually means, what you owe, when it's due, and whether anything looks off compared to your last one.

Tradie Finder

Describe the job that needs doing and get a plain checklist for finding, vetting, and pricing a tradesperson — including questions to ask so you don't get quoted a mate's rate that isn't one.
