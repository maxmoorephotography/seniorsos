About

Why SeniorsOS exists.

Most AI tools were built for people who already find computers easy. That leaves out a lot of people — including plenty of sharp, capable adults who just never had a reason to learn the jargon, and shouldn't have to.

SeniorsOS started as a simple bet: if the same AI tools that help someone draft an email or decode a bill were repackaged in plain language, with no account, no app, and no ten-minute onboarding, they'd genuinely help. Scam Shield came first, because scams aimed at older Australians are common, convincing, and rarely explained clearly until it's too late.

What we won't do

  • We won't fill the toolkit with modules nobody asked for, just to look comprehensive.
  • We won't add a product to the shop because it pays well — only because we'd hand it to our own family.
  • We won't make Scam Shield anything other than free.

Who it's for

Older adults navigating daily admin on their own, and the adult children who end up as unofficial tech support. Either way, if it saves someone a confusing phone call or a bad decision, it's done its job.

Get in touch
