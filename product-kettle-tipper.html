Kettle Tipper

Pours a full kettle with a light push instead of lifting the whole weight.

Why we like it

A full kettle gets heavy fast, and tipping it forward from the wrist is where most strain happens. This one is deliberately lightweight, with a shape and handle built for a controlled, low-effort pour rather than a full lift.

  • Lightweight build reduces strain when pouring a full kettle
  • Collapses flat for easy storage between uses
  • Auto shut-off switch for safety on the base
  • Compact size suits a smaller kitchen or a bench with limited space

Good to know

Let it cool before collapsing it flat for storage — the silicone body folds far more easily once it's not hot.
