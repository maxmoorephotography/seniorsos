Fragrance-Free Body Lotion

A thick, fast-absorbing lotion made for skin that's gotten drier and thinner with age.

Why we like it

Skin naturally produces less oil with age, which is part of why it can feel drier and thinner than it used to. Added fragrance is a common culprit for stinging or irritating skin in that state, so this one skips it entirely while still delivering a thick, moisturising formula that sinks in fast rather than sitting greasy.

  • Fragrance-free formula avoids stinging thin, sensitive skin
  • Thick, fast-absorbing texture without a greasy feel
  • Suited to daily use on face and body
  • Simple ingredient list, easy to check against sensitivities

Good to know

Apply just after showering while skin is still slightly damp — it helps the lotion absorb faster and locks in more moisture.
