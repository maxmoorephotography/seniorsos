Is Scam Shield really free forever?

Yes. It doesn't expire, and it doesn't need a card on file. It's the module most people need most urgently, so it stays free.

Is the Full Toolkit a subscription?

No — it's a one-time payment. You pay once and keep access, including any future updates to the six modules.

Can a family member use it on my behalf?

Yes. Many people set it up for a parent, or use it together. There's no per-person limit.

Do I need to know anything about AI to use it?

No. Each module already knows what to ask you — you just answer in plain language, the way you'd explain the problem to a person.

What if it doesn't help with my situation?

Get in touch through the suggestion page and tell us what happened — we'd genuinely like to know so we can improve the module.
