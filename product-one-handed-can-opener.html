One-Handed Can Opener

Clamps on and turns itself — useful if grip strength or the use of one hand is limited.

Why we like it

Standard can and jar openers usually assume two strong hands and a good grip. This one clamps around the rim and does the turning itself once it's engaged, so it can be operated one-handed without needing to grip and twist at the same time.

  • Clamps around the lid and turns itself once engaged
  • Works one-handed — no twisting or wrist strength needed
  • Stainless steel construction holds up to daily use
  • Compact enough to store in a kitchen drawer

Good to know

Make sure the clamp is fully seated around the rim before starting to turn — a half-seated grip is the most common reason it slips.
