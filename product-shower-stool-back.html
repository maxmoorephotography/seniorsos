Shower Stool with Back

Sturdy enough to lean on, low enough to feel steady getting on and off.

Why we like it

Showering seated takes the risk out of standing on a wet, slippery floor, but a wobbly stool defeats the purpose. This one has a proper backrest to lean into, and adjustable-height legs finished with non-slip suction feet, so it stays planted through the whole shower.

  • Backrest gives real support, not just a perch
  • Adjustable-height legs suit different shower setups
  • Non-slip suction feet keep it steady on wet tile
  • Drainage holes in the seat let water pass straight through

Good to know

Check the suction feet are seated flat on the shower floor before use, especially on textured tile where the seal can be weaker.
