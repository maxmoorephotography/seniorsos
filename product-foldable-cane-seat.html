Foldable Cane with Seat

Folds small enough for the car, opens into a seat when the queue is long.

Why we like it

Long queues and waiting rooms are exactly when a cane alone isn't quite enough. This one unfolds from a walking stick into a stable tripod seat in a couple of seconds, so there's always somewhere to sit without needing to plan ahead.

  • Folds between a walking stick and a portable tripod seat
  • Stainless steel frame keeps it light but sturdy
  • Ergonomic T-handle for a comfortable grip while walking
  • Compact folded size fits easily in a car or bag

Good to know

Check that all three legs are fully locked out before sitting — it only takes a second and makes the seat much steadier.
