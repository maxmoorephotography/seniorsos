Suction Grab Bar

No drilling — moves with you if you rearrange, and holds steady when it matters.

Why we like it

A lot of grab bars require drilling into tile, which puts people off installing one at all — especially in a rental. This one uses heavy-duty suction cups with a locking lever, so it goes up in seconds and comes down just as easily if you need to reposition it.

  • Locking suction levers with a visual indicator that shows a secure seal
  • No drilling, screws, or tools needed to install or remove
  • Repositionable — move it to a new spot as needs change
  • Rated to support real weight when stepping in or out

Good to know

Suction holds best on smooth tile, glass, or fibreglass. Clean the surface well before mounting, and check the seal indicator every so often.
